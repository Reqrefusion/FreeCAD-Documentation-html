Beschreibung

This tool allows you to place a view of a selected spreadsheet on a drawing sheet.

Anwendung

  1. Select a spreadsheet you wish to put on a drawing sheet
  2. Press the Drawing SpreadsheetView button

Eigenschaften

  • DatenCell Start: The top left cell of the cells range to be included in this view
  • DatenCell End: The bottom right cell of the cells range to be included in this view
  • DatenFont: The name of the font used for texts
  • DatenColor: The color of lines and texts that have no color specified in the spreadsheet
  • DatenFont Size: The font size of texts
  • DatenLine Width: The width of the cell borders
