Beschreibung

Flossen werden benutzt um die Flugrichtung aerodynamisch zu kontrollieren.

Eine konische Finne mit TTW-Lasche

Anwendung

  1. There are several ways to invoke the command:
    • Press the Fin button.
    • Select the Rocket → Fin option from the menu.
    • Double click on a Fin object in the Tree view.
  2. Set options and press OK.

Optionen

Flossen-Typen

Die allgemeine Form der Flosse.

  • Trapez.

This fin type is used for almost all 4 sided fins. The root remains fixed, but the leading and trailing edges can have variable amounts of sweep. The chord length of the root and tip are set independently.

Trapezflossentyp

  • Elliptisch.

A generalized rounded shape. Circular fins are a special case where the height is 1/2 of the root chord.

Elliptischer Flossentyp

  • Benutzerdefiniert.

Most fins will fit into one of the standard shapes. In cases where they don't it's possible to create a custom shape using a sketch.

The sketch must be created first. The fin root is drawn along the positive X axis. With the sketch selected in the model tree, selecting the Fin icon will create the custom fin. The fin profile can be changed at this time.

There is no way to associate a custom fin with a sketch within the dialog after it's been created, so it is important to create the sketch first. It can be selected from the Properties view. Updating the sketch will update the fin.

Skizze zum Erstellen einer benutzerdefinierten Flossenform

Die aus der Skizze erstellte benutzerdefinierte Flosse

Querschnitt

The cross sectional shape of a fin can greatly affect its performance at different speeds, as well as the looks of the rocket. A variety of fin cross sections have been implemented. Fins are created by lofting the root cross section to the tip cross section, so not all combinations of Root Cross Section and Tip Cross Section will produce useful fins.

  • Square. Both the leading and trailing edges are squared.

Quadratischer Querschnitt

  • Round. The leading and trailing edges are rounded.

Runder Querschnitt

  • Airfoil. Uses the NACA symmetrical airfoil shape NACA airfoil with maximum thickness at 30% of the chord.

Tragflächenquerschnitt

  • Wedge. The trailing edge of the fin is square, converging to a point at the leading edge.

Keilquerschnitt

  • Diamond. The diamond shape starts from a point at the leading edge, straight out to the maximum thickness at a point determined by Length 1, and back to a point at the trailing edge.

Diamantquerschnitt

  • Leading Edge (LE) Taper. The leading edge is tapered to a point as determined by Length 1.

Vorderkante mit konischem Querschnitt

  • Trailing Edge (TE) Taper. The trailing edge is tapered to a point as determined by Length 1.

Hinterkante mit konischem Querschnitt

  • Taper. The leading edge is tapered to a point as determined by Length 1, and the trailing edge is tapered to a point as determined by Length 2.

Konischer Querschnitt

Durch die Wand (TTW)-Lasche

Through the Wall fins add structural strength by extending though the outer body tube to an inner body tube such as a motor mount. Instead of attaching just to the outside of the outer body tube, it can be attached at multiple points. As such, the height of the tab would be the distance from the outer diameter of the inner body tube to the outer diameter of the outer body tube. Other parameters would vary depending on requirements.

Vier TTW-Flossen, die an einer zentralen Motorhalterung im äußeren Rumpfrohr befestigt sind

Eigenschaften

Flosse

  • DatenFin Type: Defines the shape of the fin.
  • DatenHeight: The fin height.
  • DatenProfile: The sketch associated with the custom fin type.
  • DatenRoot Chord: The distance between the fin leading and trailing edges at the root
  • DatenRoot Cross Section: The cross section shape of the fin at the root, see Options
  • DatenRoot Length 1: Usage depends on the Fin Root Cross Section and will apply to a taper length or similar, see Options
  • DatenRoot Length 2: Usage depends on the Fin Root Cross Section and will apply to a taper length or similar when multiple values are required, see Options
  • DatenRoot Per Cent: Expresses the Fin Root Length 1 and Fin Root Length 2 properties as a percentage of the Fin Root Chord
  • DatenRoot Thickness: Maximum thickness at the root of the fin
  • DatenSweep Angle: The angle of the front of the fin, with a vertical front being 0 degrees. This may be negative. Setting this value will cause the Sweep Length to be adjusted.
  • DatenSweep Length: The distance from the front of the fin root to the front of the fin tip along the x axis. This may be negative. Setting this value will cause the Sweep Angle to be adjusted.
  • DatenTip Chord: The distance between the fin leading and trailing edges at the tip
  • DatenTip Cross Section: The cross section shape of the fin at the tip, see Options
  • DatenTip Length 1: Usage depends on the Fin Tip Cross Section and will apply to a taper length or similar, see Options
  • DatenTip Length 2: Usage depends on the Fin Tip Cross Section and will apply to a taper length or similar when multiple values are required, see Options
  • DatenTip Per Cent: Expresses the Fin Tip Length 1 and Fin Tip Length 2 properties as a percentage of the Fin Tip Chord
  • DatenTip Thickness: Maximum thickness at the tip of the fin
  • DatenTtw: True when a tab for Through the Wall fins is required, see Options
  • DatenTtw Height: Height of the TTW tab
  • DatenTtw Length: Length of the TTW tab
  • DatenTtw Offset: Distance from the front of the fin to the front of the TTW tab
  • DatenTtw Thickness: Thickness of the TTW tab

Rocket Komponenten

Diese Parameter dienen nur zu Informationszwecken und haben keinen Einfluss auf die Konstruktion der Komponente.

  • DatenDescription: Beschreibung der Komponente
  • DatenManufacturer: Hersteller, wenn bekannt
  • DatenMaterial: Material, wenn bekannt
  • DatenPart Number: Hersteller, Teilenummer
