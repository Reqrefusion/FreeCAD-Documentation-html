Description

Removes the selected objects and all children that are not referenced from other objects.

Usage

  1. Select object(s) to remove.
  2. There are several ways to invoke the tool:
    • Press the Remove Objects and their children button.
    • Select the OpenSCAD → Remove Objects and their children option from the menu.
