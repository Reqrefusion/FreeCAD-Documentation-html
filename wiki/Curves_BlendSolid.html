Description

The Curves BlendSolid tool creates a solid between two edges with some continuity with their support shapes.

Usage

  1. Select two faces of different solids.
  2. There are several ways to invoke the tool:
    • Press the BlendSolid button.
    • Select the Surfaces → BlendSolid option from the menu.
  3. A Blend_Solid object is created.
