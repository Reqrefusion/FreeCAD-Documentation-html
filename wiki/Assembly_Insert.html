Description

The File:Assembly Insert.svg Assembly Insert tool inserts a new Part container, optionally embedded in a new document, and adds it to the currently active Assembly.

Usage

  1. There are several ways to invoke the tool:
    • Press the File:Assembly Insert.svg Insert a new part button.
    • Select the Assembly → File:Assembly Insert.svg Insert a new part option from the menu.
