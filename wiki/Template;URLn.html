Template:URLn

{{{1}}}

Use this template when you want to display a URL in monospace font without a link. To include a link, use Template:URL.

Example: 

{{URLn|https://www.freecad.org/wiki}}

{{URL|https://www.freecad.org/wiki}}

Result:

https://www.freecad.org/wiki

https://www.freecad.org/wiki
