Description

The Curves SegmentSurface allows to segment a surface on isocurves. This tool is part of the external workbench called Curves.

Usage

  1. Switch to the Curves workbench (install from Addon Manager is necessary, if not previously installed)
  2. To invoke the command, do one of the following:
    • Press the button in the Curves toolbar.
    • Use the Surfaces → Segment surface entry in the dropdown menu.
