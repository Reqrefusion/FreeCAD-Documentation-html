Description

L'outil Curves DraftAnalysis crée une superposition colorée sur un objet pour visualiser les angles de dépouille.

Une sphère avec une ligne réfléchie à son équateur (à gauche) et l'objet DraftAnalysis correspondant (à droite)

Utilisation

  1. Sélectionnez une ou plusieurs formes.
  2. Il y a plusieurs façons de lancer l'outil :
    • Appuyez sur le bouton Draft Analysis.
    • Sélectionnez l'option Surfaces → Draft Analysis du menu.
  3. Un objet DraftAnalysis est créé pour chaque forme sélectionnée.

Remarques

  • With this tool you can check if an object could be die cast in general and in connection with reflect lines determine the parting line for the die cavities, like in the above image. The tool direction runs through the poles of the sphere and its equator is the parting line. In this case the color ranges above and below the parting line can be considered separate analyses of both halves.
  • If this tool is applied to an object representing a die then one range analyses how well a cast item would detach from the die while the other highlightes the regions that would obstruct the ejection of the item (undercuts).
  • The angles should be set according to manufacturing process and material chosen for the selected item. For example:
    • VueDraft Angle1+2: The minimum required angle that would still result in proper parts if other settings of the manufacturing process are fine-tuned and monitored.
    • VueDraft Tol1+2: Added on top of the required angle giving the minimum recommended angle that will result in proper parts and a stable manufacturing process under any circumstance without further action.
  • The colors:
    • VueColor In Draft1+2: Marks areas with draft angles larger than the recommended angle.
    • VueColor In Tolerance1+2: Marks areas with draft angles between the required angle and the minimum recommended angle.
    • VueColor Out Of Draft1+2: Marks areas with draft angles below the required angle that can be interpreted as having no draft at all.

Properties

See also: Property editor.

A DraftAnalysis object is derived from an App FeaturePython object and inherits all its properties. It also has the following additional properties:

View

Analysis Options

  • VueDirection (Vector): Anaysis direction
  • VueDraft Angle1 (FloatConstraint): Positive draft angle
  • VueDraft Angle2 (FloatConstraint): Negative draft angle
  • VueDraft Tol1 (FloatConstraint): Positive draft tolerance
  • VueDraft Tol2 (FloatConstraint): Negative draft tolerance
  • VueShading (FloatConstraint): Amount of shading on the analysis overlay

Colors1 Positive Draft

  • VueColor In Draft1 (Color): Color of the positive in-draft area
  • VueColor In Tolerance1 (Color): Color of the positive tolerance area
  • VueColor Out Of Draft1 (Color): Color of the positive out-of-draft area

Colors2 Negative Draft

  • VueColor In Draft2 (Color): Color of the negative in-draft area
  • VueColor In Tolerance2 (Color): Color of the negative tolerance area
  • VueColor Out Of Draft2 (Color): Color of the negative out-of-draft area

Data

Analysis Options

  • DonnéesSource (Link): Object on which the analysis is performed
