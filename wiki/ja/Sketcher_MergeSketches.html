概要

The Sketcher MergeSketches tool merges two or more sketches.

使い方

  1. Select two or more sketches.
  2. There are several ways to invoke the tool:
    • Press the Merge Sketches button (not available in the PartDesign Workbench).
    • Select the Sketch → Merge Sketches option from the menu.
  3. A new sketch is created.
