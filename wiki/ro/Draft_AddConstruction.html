Descriere

Această comandaă adaugă obiectele selectate la Draft Construction group.

Cum se folosește

  1. Selectați unele obiecte
  2. Faceți clic pe meniul Proiect → Utilitare → Add to construction group

Notes

  • Objects can also be added to the construction group by drag and dropping them on the group in the Tree View, or by using the Draft AddToGroup command. But in both cases the construction geometry color is not applied.
  • For more information about organizing your model see Document structure and Arch tutorial.
