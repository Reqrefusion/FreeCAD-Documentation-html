Description

Lets you override one or more properties of a trajectory.

Usage

  1. Select trajectory or Edge2Trac-trajectory to be dressed up in the Tree view
  2. Click on or change to the Task panel and click on Dress-up trajectory

Resulting in the ability to change values for:

  • speed
  • acceleration
  • continous mode
  • position and orientation
