Template:APIClass b

{{{1}}}({{{2}}})

This template is like Template:APIClass but does not include the description. It's meant to show different arguments.

Usage: 

{{APIClass_b|class name|arguments with defaults}}
{{APIClass  |class name|arguments with defaults|long description}}

Example: 

{{APIClass_b|BigClass|A, B}}
{{APIClass  |BigClass|Vector|uses the points ''A'' and ''B'', or a ''Vector''}}
BigClass(A, B)
BigClass(Vector)

Description: uses the points A and B, or a Vector
