Use this template when you want to indicate a button that you must press in the graphical user interface of the program.

Example: 

{{Button|select edge}} {{Button|Cancel}} {{Button|OK}} {{Button|[[Image:Sketcher_Snap.svg|16px]] [[Sketcher_Snap|Toggle snap]]}}
