The main areas of weakness i find with FreeCad vs other cad software ( im comparing to best of the best and that shows how much belief i have so dont take this as negative at all)

Surfacing so far it just looks like a work bench created by other users, it would be great to see proper surfacing tools like the NX tools through curve mesh with continuity and tangency constraints to go along with it and just in general combine curve projection - while something similar has been made its a bit clunky and feels unpolished. And the airfoil tool while cool doesnt have enough options and it isnt allowed to be taken further into modelling trees which i find annoying. As well as any other functions bounded plains sew fill, surface modelling is amazing and its whats stopping me from loving this software totally - as a designer myself.

Modelling layouts - i love NX double click that activates a body and takes everything else to be slightly transparent as well as taking evertyhing that was made during this inside the active body . Also while i see what is being done in having sketches linked under a body i prefer nx in having only one sketch at the start and then the bodies being generated without the drop down and linked sketch inside - as well as feature groups that dont have any impact on anything just allow nicer organisation.

Extrude - i also love how many functions are within nx extrude - i know this may be too complex for standard users - but just please create an option to toggle expert mode or somehintg and within extrude add things like start point and end point so i can create a body that is totally offset from the sketch with on function - also loft angle within would be nice.

Loading functionality - i have started making large assemblies and ti would

be nice to do something like nx to start off with minimal detail for surrounding bodies and acitve body to be fully loaded olnly otherwise it gets very laggy and i need to change or hide all other bodies and lose conteext


I know some of these may already exist and i apologise but they clearly arent covered enough or sufficiently simple for me to find out about
